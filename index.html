Artificial intelligence (AI) has come a long way in the last few years and is changing how we live. But did you know that AI can also cause problems for society? It's true, and here's why.

One of the things that worries people is that AI can make existing problems worse. For example, if the data used to train an AI is biased, the AI will end up discriminating against certain groups of people. This can make things unfair for women, people of color, and other marginalized groups.

Another thing that worries people is that AI can take away jobs from people. While it can make things faster and more efficient, it can also automate jobs that are currently done by humans. This can cause job loss and financial difficulties, especially for people who work in low-skilled jobs.

AI can also be used for bad things, like cybercrime and cyberattacks. AI-powered bots can spread lies and propaganda on social media, and AI can be used to automate cyberattacks and break into computer systems. This can lead to people being taken advantage of and important information being stolen.

Lastly, AI can be used to watch people and control what they do. This means that surveillance systems can monitor people's activities, which could invade their privacy and take away their freedom. Additionally, AI-powered systems can be used in law enforcement and criminal justice, which can lead to unfair and biased decisions.

In conclusion, while AI can be helpful, it can also cause problems for society. It's important that we think about these concerns and work to make sure AI is used responsibly and fairly.

Artificial Intelligence is Destroying Local Businesses

by Brodie Lewis

May 30, 2023

In the previous article, we discussed how artificial intelligence (AI) has the potential to cause harm to society. Unfortunately, we are already seeing some of these negative impacts play out in real life, and the consequences are devastating for small business owners.

Take my family's cooking business, for example. We've been running our restaurant for generations, serving up delicious home-cooked meals to the community. However, in recent years, the rise of AI-powered robot chefs has completely decimated our business. These machines are faster, more efficient, and cheaper than human workers, and they never make mistakes or take breaks.

At first, we tried to compete by improving our menu and customer service, but it wasn't enough. People were drawn to the novelty and efficiency of the robot chefs, and our business suffered. We were forced to lay off our employees, including our long-time chefs who had dedicated their lives to perfecting our family recipes.

The impact of AI on our business is just a small example of the larger issue at hand. As AI becomes more advanced and ubiquitous, it has the potential to completely upend entire industries and destroy the livelihoods of millions of workers. It's not just restaurants that are at risk - it's any business that relies on human labor.

Furthermore, the rise of AI-powered automation is exacerbating economic inequality and concentrating wealth in the hands of a few. The people who own and operate these machines will become increasingly wealthy and powerful, while those who are displaced by them will be left behind.

In this dystopian future, where AI has taken over our jobs and our livelihoods, it's crucial that we come together as a society to address these issues. We need to think about how we can ensure that AI is used responsibly and ethically, and how we can support workers who are displaced by these technological advances.

The rise of AI has led to a massive concentration of power in the hands of a few tech giants. The consequences of this are terrifying, and we are only beginning to scratch the surface of what AI is capable of.
